Project Overview

Gensyn is a decentralized AI compute marketplace designed to break the monopoly of centralized cloud providers. It connects global GPU/TPU resources, enabling AI developers to directly access and pay for training compute, with all rewards and validation enforced on-chain.

Core Features

  • Open Market: Anyone can contribute or rent GPU resources, reducing entry barriers.
  • On-chain Incentives: All rewards and tasks are settled transparently via the GSY token.
  • Distributed Verification: Protects against fake compute, ensures quality AI training.

GSY Tokenomics

  • Utility: Used for AI task payments, node incentives, and governance.
  • Token Allocation: Airdrops, team, ecosystem, and investors; initial inflation drives adoption.
  • Value Drivers: More training jobs and active compute demand mean higher intrinsic GSY value.
