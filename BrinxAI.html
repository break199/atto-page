1. Overview

  • BrinxAI is building a distributed AI compute & data network, integrating idle GPUs/CPUs and valuable datasets for AI+Web3 infra.
  • Anyone can deploy worker nodes (open-source on GitHub), contribute compute/data, and share rewards for AI training/inference tasks.
  • Competing with io.net, Aethir, TAO, with a unique dual-incentive model for both data and compute.

2. Tech Highlights

  • Open-source worker node architecture for permissionless participation.
  • On-chain task matching, native $BRINX token for rewards & DAO governance.
  • Compute & data marketplace, tools for developers & AI Dapps.

  • Run a worker node, complete tasks, and earn points for OG, whitelist, and future $BRINX airdrop.
